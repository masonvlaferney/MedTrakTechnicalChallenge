Here are the rules of Farkle: On your turn, you roll six dice. Points are earned every time you roll a 1, 5, or three of a kind.

If none of the dice rolled earned points, that's a Farkle! Your turn is over and you earn no points. If you roll at least one scoring die, you can either bank your point total and pass your turn, or you can risk these points by putting aside the dice you've scored (at least one) and continuing to roll the remaining dice. The remaining dice may earn you additional points, but if you Farkle, you lose everything you've earned during your turn. Scoring is based only on the dice in each roll. You cannot earn points by combining dice from different rolls. You can continue rolling the dice until you either Pass or Farkle
